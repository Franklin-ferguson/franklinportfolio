About

Who am I?

I am an Aerospace Engineering graduate specializing in UAV design, robotics, and computational fluid dynamics (CFD), with a passion for advancing technologies that bridge research and real-world applications. My expertise spans drone operations, system integration, and data-driven decision support, where I have delivered innovative solutions for agriculture, afforestation, surveillance, and environmental monitoring.

Beyond engineering, I am committed to STEM education and mentorship, leading drone workshops in schools to inspire the next generation of innovators. My professional journey reflects a blend of technical expertise, research-driven problem solving, and leadership, aimed at pushing the boundaries of aerospace technology while creating meaningful impact across industries and communities.
